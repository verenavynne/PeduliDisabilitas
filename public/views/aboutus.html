About Us

Welcome to PeduliDisabilitas!

PeduliDisabilitas is a marketplace dedicated to showcasing and selling unique products created by talented individuals with disabilities. Our platform not only highlights their craftsmanship but also empowers them to achieve financial independence.

Vision

To create a world where every individual with a disability is recognized for their talents and has equal opportunities to succeed in the marketplace

Mission

To provide a supportive platform that enables individuals with disabilities to showcase their products, enhance their skills, and connect with customers who value their creativity and hard work

Values

  • Empowerment
  • Inclusion
  • Quality
  • Respect

Why PeduliDisabilitas?

We believe that everyone deserves an opportunity to shine, regardless of their abilities. By creating this marketplace, we aim to:

  • Highlight Talent: Showcase the incredible skills and creativity of individuals with disabilities.
  • Foster Inclusion: Build a community that values diversity and promotes equal opportunities.
  • Support Financial Independence: Help individuals gain financial stability through their craftsmanship.
